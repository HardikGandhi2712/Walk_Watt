Our Story

At this hackathon, our team focused on two critical services aimed at transforming urban infrastructure. First, we developed a traffic data-driven analytics system that uses real-time data to dynamically adjust traffic signal timers, ensuring smoother traffic flow and reducing congestion across busy intersections. By leveraging machine learning, our solution can optimize traffic patterns, cutting down on commute times and reducing emissions. Our second initiative centered on sustainability: we designed energy-harvesting tiles that convert the kinetic energy from footsteps into electricity. Placed in high-footfall areas like malls, train stations, and parks, these tiles generate clean energy to power public utilities such as streetlights and charging stations. Together, these innovations contribute to building a smarter, more sustainable city, where technology adapts to human activity and helps reduce environmental impact.

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission is to create intelligent, sustainable urban solutions by leveraging data and innovative technologies. We aim to enhance the quality of life in cities by optimizing infrastructure for efficiency, reducing environmental impact, and fostering renewable energy generation through everyday activities. Our focus is on transforming urban mobility and energy systems to build smarter, more adaptive cities that work seamlessly for the people who live and move within them. We envision a future where cities are powered by data and human movement, seamlessly integrating advanced analytics and renewable energy solutions. Our goal is to build cities that are not only smarter and more efficient but also sustainable and responsive to the needs of their citizens, creating a harmonious balance between technology, energy, and urban life for a better, greener future.
