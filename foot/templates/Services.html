Our Services

Kinetic Electricity Generator

This innovative technology captures the kinetic energy from footsteps as people walk on specially designed energy-generating tiles. As each step is taken, the tiles convert the kinetic energy into electricity, which can be used to power various devices and public utilities. This eco-friendly solution is ideal for high-traffic areas like malls, airports, and parks, contributing to a sustainable and energy-efficient future.

Traffic Monitoring Analysis

This technology uses real-time data from sensors and cameras to optimize traffic signal timings at busy intersections. By analyzing the flow of vehicles and pedestrians, it ensures smoother traffic management, reduces congestion, and improves overall urban mobility. This smart solution dynamically adjusts traffic lights to respond to changing conditions, contributing to a more efficient and responsive transportation system in modern cities.
